About us

The Interdisciplinary Center (IDC) Herzliya is Israel's first privately-funded not-for-profit fully-accredited institution of higher education. Founded by renowned Israeli Professor Uriel Reichman in 1994, IDC Herzliya is modeled after Ivy League universities in the U.S. In the 25 years since its establishment, the success of IDC Herzliya has drawn both international recognition and some of the finest lecturers and researchers from around the world to its doorstep.

Entrepreneurship has been an integral part of IDC where it has been taught for almost 20 years. Two years ago, IDC became the first and only academic institution in Israel to offer a BA in entrepreneurship. This program and many other entrepreneurship programs such as the prestigious Zell and IDCBeyond, are housed in the Adelson School of Entrepreneurship (ASE). ASE provides students with the training, expertise, and resources needed to launch the next generation of successful Israeli ventures, while strengthening and cultivating the country’s startup ecosystem. It offers undergraduate and graduate-level experiential learning that combines academics with hands-on entrepreneurship opportunities beyond the classroom.

Le Wagon is a 9-week full-stack coding bootcamp based out of 30 cities in 20 countries. We bring coding skills to creative people who aim to create their own startup, or add a technical know-how to their skillset. No. 1 coding bootcamp worldwide for the last 3 years.
